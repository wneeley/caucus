Where is your caucus?

You are in luck, a caucus is not in a deep dark closet! So, no need to try to dig around through your old junk to find it. The location of your caucus is determined by where you live. Your city, town or neighborhood will be divided into small geographical areas called precincts. Each precinct will hold their own caucus, and they will notify community members with signs, flyers or possibly emails about when and where the caucus will be held.
